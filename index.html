about

Eritrea is a young African country, with a rich cultural histories. There are nine different ethnic groups with their own launguge and culture.

Location

Eritrea is located in the horn of Africa, bordering with neigbouring countries of Sudan, Djubuti, ethiopia and the redsea.

Due to it's of high importance strategic location, Eritrea has been under many colonizers. In 1991 after along bloody war for independence the Eritreans defeated Ethiopian Derg and anounced their independence world wide.

Eritrea has nine different ethnic groups, with the "Tigrigna" ethnic group being the largest one. Covering more than half of the population.
